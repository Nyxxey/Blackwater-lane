WHO WE ARE

About the Department

⚖

Our Mission

The Blackwater Police Department is dedicated to maintaining public safety, upholding the law, and serving the citizens of Blackwater with integrity and honor.

⚙

Our History

Founded in 1923, Blackwater PD has stood as a pillar of justice for over a century. From the shadows of the past, we forge a safer tomorrow.

★

Our Values

Honor. Duty. Vigilance. Every officer carries these principles into the dark streets they patrol, ensuring no citizen walks alone.
